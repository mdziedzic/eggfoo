Syllogism

Consultancy Website & Blog







What started out as an interactive Flash site for this London-based consultancy morphed into one built with HTML/CSS/JavaScript/jQuery in order to meet Britain's more demanding accessibility requirements. In the end, we were able to achieve Level AA of the W3C's Accessibility Guidelines while still managing to create a site that feels responsive. Sadly, some hair was lost in the process.


Deliverables

Logo and branding. UX/UI/visual design. Front-end development (HTML/CSS/JavaScript/jQuery). WordPress integration. Hosting.
